About

Welcome to Mademoiselleangel Amore - bakes she loves!

Inspired by the serenity of enjoying a cup of tea with french financiers, Mademoiselleangel Amore is created to share all the beautiful bakes she loves to her family and friends in lovely-wrapped presents. Baking empowers me to do something more and beautiful that makes me happy! Every bake is special and empowering - with a spirit of power, of love, and of a sound mind, I foraged into the baking adventure step by step, and it turned out to be the most enriching time of baking and sharing my french financiers that brings joy and sweetness to everyone!
