About

Welcome to Mademoiselleangel - bakes she loves!

Inspired by the serenity of enjoying a cup of tea with french financiers, Mademoiselleangel Amore is created to share all the beautiful bakes she loves with her family and friends in lovely-wrapped presents.

Welcome to my shop! I am Angel, who loves to explore the sweetest things in life. Baking french financiers is one of the most amazing skills to do just that! Baking empowers me to do something more beautiful that makes me happy! With a spirit of power, of love, and of a sound mind, I foraged into the baking adventure step by step, and it turns out to be the most rewarding time of baking and sharing my french financiers that brings joy and sweetness to everyone!

Ordering

Being a home business, managing bulk orders at any time of the day and week can be much hassle, along with the clashing of personal schedules. Thus, a monthly ordering cycle has been adopted, where all products ordered within the month's cycle will be collected at the end of that particular month cycle. Hence, please ensure your order is made within the one month cycle before the expected date of collection. Continue reading to learn more about our ordering cycle.

Ordering Cycle

Our ordering cycle involves a standard four weeks before collection. Our orders are not in a first-come-first-serve basis. Order anytime within this cycle and they will be ready at the end of the cycle!
